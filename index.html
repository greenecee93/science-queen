About Ms. Greene

Science is more than memorizing facts—it's learning how to think. I meet students where they are, build strong foundations, and connect ideas so they stick.

Teaching philosophy

I use everyday analogies, guided inquiry, and scaffolded problem sets. Students learn to explain concepts in their own words, then apply them in increasingly complex scenarios.

Results you can expect

Improved test scores, less anxiety, and stronger study habits. I share short feedback notes after sessions so families see progress and next steps.

What makes this different

Blending conceptual clarity with strategic test skills—so students don't just know the "what," they master the "why" and the "how."
